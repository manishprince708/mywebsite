Welcome to LOCUS CLASSES

The source of free education

Locus Classes was founded for a noble cause—a purpose to change the way education is perceived and delivered. It started as a small organization a few years ago with a purpose of glamorizing the teaching profession and attracting the most talented teachers towards education. Over the years, many professionals, who have left their stereotypical corporate jobs to search for a purpose in life, have joined Locus Classes. All teachers at Locus Classes proudly say, “We are Teachers by choice!” The word teacher describes every team member of Locus Classes, be they from the Teaching Team or the Non-Teaching Team, as they facilitate the right kind of education to the students together. As an organization, Locus Classes has a simple HR philosophy, “Team first!!!,” because only if the team is happy and content would it be able to enjoy work and guide students towards success. Locus Classes has developed a very strong work ethic over a period of time. They aim for perfection in all undertaken tasks and have developed a culture that facilitates this.
